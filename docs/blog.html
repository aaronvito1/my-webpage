Microvision (MVIS) Stock Update

March 18, 2021 by Aaron Vito

Microvision, a company that focuses on LiDAR technology, has been making strides in the tech world, as well as for those who are interested in the stock market. This company and their products are pioneers with using lasers as a way for detection and to display interactive images. As a new shareholder of this company, I have learned that many companies would want to potentially buy the company. Some speculate this technology to be used for autonomous vehicles, so companies such as Ford, Tesla, and maybe even Apple, may purchase MVIS technology. Hopefully there will be positive news when their April annoucements arrives.
For more information, please check out Microvision for more details.
